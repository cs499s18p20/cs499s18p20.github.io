Conclusions


Developing a card recognition model is hard. There are so many variables to consider that make this a tough task. Getting quality training data, using optimal settings on Google Cloud Platform's machine learning engine, and making a quality application are all tall tasks. One could make this project a research thesis if they wanted to develop a robust model and application. We have encountered many settings and made many choices in developing this model that affects the product quality. Due to time constraints and the sheer volume of possible development decisions, we were not able to fully explore all model development choices. This was a great experience in an exciting technology field.
