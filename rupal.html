Week 2

Baby Steps

This week I was finally able to complete the TensorFlow tutorial mentioned in last week's update. Thanks to Donald's suggestion, I used the Google Cloud Shell to finish working through the steps of the tutorial. This allowed us to become more fimiliar with TensorFlow and TensorBoard, as well as get an idea of how to train and test on annotated images.

The rest of the week mainly consisted of data collection. We focused on collecting images of four cards: the ace of diamonds, the king of hearts, the queen of hearts, and the jack of hearts. We each took 60 pictures of each card in different angles and positions on varying backgrounds. This is in order to create a very diverse training set, allowing for image recognition to be more inclusive. After taking the various images, I labeled each one using an LabelImg, a prgram created solely to annotate images. Our hope is to repeat this process during the upcoming weeks for more cards.
